Core Services & Technologies

Paintly AI

Paintly, an AI-based video styler offering near-limitless possibilities, is one of the most advanced AI styling solutions available today. Based on AIANETWORKS' proprietary AI style transfer model, it delicately preserves the characteristics of original content while quickly and accurately applying various artistic styles desired by the user, creating new visual experiences. Its One Source Multi-Use feature allows for the generation of diverse outputs from a single source.

Key Technologies: Proprietary AI Style Transfer Model, One Source Multi-Use

Applications: Film, Webtoons, Digital Art, Advertising, Personalized Goods Production, etc.

New Project: Caret

AIANETWORKS is collaborating with its partner, Caretive, to innovate the AI development ecosystem. Caretive is dedicated to the technological development of the next-generation AI development agent, 'Caret,' and AIANETWORKS is working with Caretive to create synergy in this new business area. Furthermore, AIANETWORKS aims to contribute to the popularization of AI technology by conducting B2C AI coding education and AI art education businesses utilizing Caretive's 'Caret.' In particular, we plan to provide a more friendly and immersive customized educational experience for learners through Caret's persona setting function.

Caret is an open-source project aiming for developer-led orchestration, freedom of model choice, and more.

Key Features: Developer-led orchestration using .caretrules, aiming for multi-agent and RAG support, flexibility in choosing various LLMs (OpenAI, Groq, Gemini, local sLLMs, etc.).

GitHub Repository: https://github.com/aicoding-caret/caret-zero
